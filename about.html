About Us

FETS Energy Solutions is a leading provider of cutting-edge solar energy solutions in Calicut. We offer a wide range of products, including solar cells, inverters,home theaters,home automation, UPS systems, and batteries, to help you harness the power of the sun. Our products are designed to be highly efficient, reliable, and eco-friendly, allowing you to save money and make a positive impact on the environment. Our team of experts is dedicated to providing exceptional customer service, ensuring that you receive the support you need to make the most of your investment. Whether you're looking to power your home, business, or community, Vectrex has the solution you need to build a brighter, more sustainable future.

Mission

Our mission at FETS is to bring power to your life in a sustainable way. We aim to provide innovative energy solutions that improve the quality of life for our customers while having a positive impact on the environment. We strive to make clean, reliable, and affordable energy accessible to everyone, creating a brighter future for all.We invest in R&D,collaborate with stakeholders, and foster a culture of sustainability throughout our businesses and communities to fulfil our mission.

Vision

At FETS, our vision is to create a world powered by clean and efficient energy. We envision a future where renewable energy is accessible to everyone, reducing our dependence on non-renewable sources and creating a more sustainable planet for future generations. Our goal is to be the leading provider of cutting-edge solar energy solutions, empowering individuals, communities, and businesses to achieve energy independence and make a positive impact on the environment.
